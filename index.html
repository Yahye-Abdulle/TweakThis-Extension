Want to share your Netflix List to anyone?

  • I have developed a Google Chrome Extension that will allow you to share your complete list to anyone with an active sharing code with unlimited uses.

How To Install and Use

  • 1. Head over to the Google Chrome Extension Store
  • 2. Search Share My List
  • 3. Add to Chrome and Head over to Netflix.com/browse/my-list
  • 4. Refresh page, share button will appear and click on it to receieve sharing code!
